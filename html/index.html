About Us

The restaurant is spacious and full of light, the perfect place to enjoy a meal with friends or family, or stop in for a drink at the end of the day.Both the selection of the location and the design of the interior required careful preparation. After nearly 5 years of planning and construction, we opened our restaurant for gourmets in downtown Budapest, in September 2018. Apart from the unique culinary spectacles we provide, our guests can admire the special shapes and colours of interior spaces, as well.

Our menu includes a wide range of traditional and southern Italian specialities. Our buffet table offers a great variety of Italian starters, desserts, fresh pastas, grilled dishes and ready-to-serve meals. Our salad bar offers fresh vegetables, green salads and delicious dressings. Our focaccia is baked in our own oven only with selected high-quality ingredients

All of our pastas are made in the Restaurant based on our own recipes. Our pasta dishes are made a la minute upon request. As our pizzas are baked from crispy, thin dough in wood-fired oven, they preserve traditional tastes.A wide range of fresh meat and vegetable dishes, including chicken and seafood -depending on the time of the day- are grilled on lava stone. Our desserts include classic Italian confectioneries and own recipes. Some of our pasta dishes, pizzas and desserts are available in gluten-free version.
